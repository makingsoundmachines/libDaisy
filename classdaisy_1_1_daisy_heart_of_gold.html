Detailed Description

Class that handles initializing all of the hardware specific to the Heart of Gold, based on the Daisy Patch Board.
Helper funtions are also in place to provide easy access to built-in controls and peripherals.

Author
Stephen Hensley, Making Sound Machines
Date
March 2020
